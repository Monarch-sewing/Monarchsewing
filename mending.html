Mending Services

Giving your favorite garments a second life.

Your favorite clothes deserve a second chance

Whether it is a family heirloom, a high-end designer piece, or that perfectly worn-in pair of jeans, we specialize in repairing the damage while honoring the history of the garment. From "invisible" fixes to decorative art, we mend with precision.

Invisible Mending

The elite choice for suits and fine woolens. We meticulously re-weave threads back into the original fabric to make holes and tears virtually disappear.

Decorative Darning

Based on the Japanese Sashiko method. We turn a tear into a work of art using beautiful contrasting threads, making your garment unique and stronger than before.

Structural Repair

Replacing broken zippers, reinforcing thinning elbow patches, or fixing split seams in leather and heavy denim. Built for durability and daily wear.
